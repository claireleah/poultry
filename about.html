About Us

At Golden Feathers Poultry Farm, we believe in raising our chickens with care, love, and dedication. Established in 2010, our farm has grown from a small backyard coop to one of the most trusted sources of fresh eggs and healthy poultry in the region.

Our team works tirelessly to ensure our birds are fed with high-quality, organic feed and have access to clean water and spacious living conditions. This not only ensures the health of our chickens but also guarantees the quality and taste of our products.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, healthy, and affordable poultry products to our community while promoting sustainable farming practices that respect both the animals and the environment.
